We believe that access to the Internet is a human right.

Those without the skills to protect themselves online are at risk of social and economic disadvantage. Public services and essential information are increasingly moving online, and many people are unable to access those resources. In 2019, The Together Institute was started to build a safer and more accessible internet experience.
